Main Objective: Create a program that will suggest how many bonbons the user should pack and how many boxes they will produce. There are two types of chocolate bonbons, Almond and peanut butter, as well as two types of boxes, round and oval.

BonBon “A” - Almond
BonBon “B” - Peanut Butter
Oval Box - 11 pieces of chocolate “A”, 4 pieces of chocolate “B”.
Round Box - 7 pieces of chocolate “A”, 4 pieces of chocolate “B”.

First, I will greet the user by asking for their first and last name.
